  • About
    This site lets you search for (your favourite) Marvel characters, and gets information for them from Marvel's database.
    You will be able to see which comics the characters were in, which series and which events they were a part of.
    This site was made to familiarise myself with obtaining data from an external source, and then presenting it in a readable fashion. Having grown up on Marvel comics, the choice was obvious!
    In all, there are 1491 entries for you to explore... Who'd have thought!
    The auto-completion will help find you a character you never heard of before today!
  • Notes
    Searches can take some time (looking at you Spider-Man!), as all data is retrieved from the Marvel database.
    I have made sure the overall wait is limited however. But do keep in mind, some searches are more extensive.
    For the moment, the results returned per character are limited to the comics and series they were in during 2018, to ensure that results are returned in a timely manner.
    In time, functionality will be added to select a specific year along with a character.
    Indicators show whether or not the content can be viewed. Unavailable content will be visibly disabled. Since data is retrieved dynamically, I have opted to get the character, comics, series and events in separate searches, to not have you wait too long in some cases.
    Any of the images retrieved can be clicked, to zoom in for a detailed view. In a collection of images like those in the list of comics, next and previous items can be selected.
